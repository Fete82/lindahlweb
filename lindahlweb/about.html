Design Philosophy

Beauty

Our attention is instinctively drawn to objects which we percieve as beautiful.

Beauty can thus be used as a tool for capturing people's attention. Often times the things we find most beautiful are seemingly simple things; a sunset, for instance, is something we are all familiar with, yet often something we also find attractive.

Simplicity

I believe simplicity is closely related to efficiency.

The efficiency of a web page is determined by the speed at which it connects a consumer with a product. It is therefore paramount that your web page is properly set up to correctly and advantageously reflect the core of your business, in an as quick a fashion as possible.

Functionality

A beautiful object is often one which performs its inherent function or purpose effectively. A knife, for instance, can be beautiful by virtue of its ability to cut. Similarly, a web page can be beautiful by virtue of fulfilling its purpose; to connect the user with the object or product displayed.

The goal of Lindahl Web is to produce simplistic, beautiful and effective web pages for our customers.
